Privacy Policy

Smart Rewrite Chrome Extension

Our Commitment to Privacy

Smart Rewrite is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Key Promise: Your API keys and text content are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers. We have no access to your personal data or the content you rewrite.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

  • API Keys: Your Groq API key is stored securely in Chrome's synchronized storage
  • Usage Statistics: Basic usage counts (number of rewrites, preferred styles) stored locally
  • Settings: Your preferences and configuration options

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Your personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • The text content you select and rewrite
  • Your browsing history or website data
  • Any analytics or tracking data
  • Your location or device information

How We Use Your Information

Local Processing

All data processing happens locally in your browser:

  • API keys are used only to authenticate with Groq's services
  • Usage statistics help improve your experience within the extension
  • Settings are used to customize the extension's behavior

Third-Party Services

When you use Smart Rewrite, your selected text is sent directly from your browser to Groq's API for processing. We act as a pass-through and never see or store this content.

Important: Your text is processed by Groq AI according to their privacy policy. Please review Groq's privacy policy at groq.com for information about how they handle data.

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • API keys are stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • Data is synchronized across your Chrome browsers when signed in
  • No data is transmitted to external servers (except Groq for processing)

Communication Security

  • All communications with Groq's API use HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are transmitted securely using standard OAuth practices
  • No persistent connections or data caching on external servers

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Delete Data: Use the "Clear Key" button to remove your API key
  • Export Data: Use the "Export Settings" feature to download your data
  • Reset Extension: Uninstall and reinstall to clear all local data

Chrome Sync

If you have Chrome Sync enabled, your API key and settings will sync across your Chrome browsers. You can disable this in Chrome's settings if preferred.

Third-Party Services

Groq AI

Smart Rewrite integrates with Groq's AI services to provide text rewriting capabilities. When you use the extension:

  • Your selected text is sent directly to Groq's API
  • Groq processes the text and returns the rewritten version
  • We do not store or access this communication
  • Groq's privacy policy applies to this data processing

No Other Third Parties

Smart Rewrite does not integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date below
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the extension
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Legal Compliance

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, Smart Rewrite complies with GDPR requirements:

  • Data Minimization: We collect only essential data for functionality
  • Purpose Limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes
  • Storage Limitation: Data is stored locally and under your control
  • User Rights: You can access, modify, and delete your data at any time

CCPA Compliance

For users in California, we provide the following rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect (outlined above)
  • Right to delete personal information (via extension settings)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Children's Privacy

Smart Rewrite is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
